## C1 🏛️ 個人自由 vs 社會責任
*← [Clusters: C1](IELTS_Topic_Clusters.md#c1--個人自由-vs-社會責任)*

**核心題：** Smoking in public places should be banned. Some say it protects health; others say it limits freedom. Discuss.

### 📥 詞彙速查（直接填入 ｜ [→ 完整詞庫 §1](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#§1-九大-hypernym-家族--萬用句型)）

| 填入位置 | 可直接使用的詞 | §1 家族 |
|---|---|---|
| `{政府/機構}` | the government / authorities / policymakers / regulators | [🏛️制度](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策) |
| `{法律/規定}` | regulation / legislation / a ban / restriction / policy | [🏛️制度](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策) |
| `{有害行為}` | smoking / excessive drinking / unsafe practices | [⚖️倫理](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-8-倫理與自由) |
| `{弱勢族群}` | children / the elderly / low-income families / non-smokers | [👤群體](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-2-受影響群體) |
| `{個人}` | citizens / individuals / taxpayers / adults | [👤群體](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-2-受影響群體) |
| `{自由選擇}` | personal freedom / autonomy / individual rights / choice | [⚖️倫理](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-8-倫理與自由) |
| `{外部代價}` | healthcare costs / social harm / public burden / second-hand effects | [🏛️制度](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策) |
| `{折衷手段}` | awareness campaigns / tax incentives / designated zones / education | [🏛️制度](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策) |

### ✍️ 段落引擎（公式 + 填法示範）

**Body 1 — [E2「造成傷害」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e2造成傷害)**
> 公式：`It damages {group's aspect} when {cause}, leading to {negative consequence}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *It damages **children's respiratory health** when passive smoking is permitted in public spaces, leading to higher rates of **asthma and lung disease** among non-smokers.*

**Body 2 — [E11「反方觀點」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e11反方觀點)**
> 公式：`The opposing view argues that {counter-argument}, but this overlooks {rebuttal}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *The opposing view argues that **a smoking ban infringes on personal freedom**, but this overlooks the fact that **one person's right to smoke ends where another's right to breathe begins**.*

**Conclusion — [E10「折衷平衡」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e10折衷平衡)**
> 公式：`Rather than {extreme A or B}, a balanced approach would be to {middle ground}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *Rather than an **outright ban** that alienates smokers, a balanced approach would be to **designate smoking zones** while implementing strict **legislation** protecting smoke-free areas for vulnerable groups.*

**Extra — [E8「政府必須」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e8政府必須)**
> 公式：`The government must {policy action} and {complement} to {outcome}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *The government must **enforce clear zoning laws** and fund **anti-smoking education campaigns** to reduce dependence without punishing individual choice.*

## C2 🤖 科技的雙面刃
*← [Clusters: C2](IELTS_Topic_Clusters.md#c2--科技的雙面刃)*

**核心題：** Some images and videos online are created by AI. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

### 📥 詞彙速查

| 填入位置 | 可直接使用的詞 | §1 家族 |
|---|---|---|
| `{科技形式}` | AI / automation / algorithms / machine learning / robots | [📱科技](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-6-科技與數位) |
| `{正面效益}` | efficiency / productivity / accessibility / innovation / speed | [💰經濟](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-3-經濟與資源) |
| `{就業影響}` | job creation / structural unemployment / skills mismatch / retraining | [💰經濟](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-3-經濟與資源) |
| `{受害群體}` | low-skilled workers / journalists / designers / mid-level professionals | [👤群體](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-2-受影響群體) |
| `{受益群體}` | students / remote communities / small businesses / elderly users | [👤群體](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-2-受影響群體) |
| `{安全/隱私}` | data privacy / surveillance / personal information / cybercrime | [📱科技](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-6-科技與數位) |
| `{解決方案}` | digital literacy / retraining programmes / regulation / ethical guidelines | [🏛️制度](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策) |
| `{統計}` | billions of users / fastest-growing sector / trillions in GDP | [💰經濟](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-3-經濟與資源) |

### ✍️ 段落引擎

**Body 1（優點）— [E1「改善生活」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e1改善生活)**
> 公式：`{X} makes {people's} life more {adj} because they can {benefit}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *AI-generated content makes **creative professionals' work** more **efficient** because they can prototype designs and draft scripts **in hours rather than weeks**, freeing time for higher-level creative decisions.*

**Body 2（缺點）— [E2「造成傷害」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e2造成傷害)**
> 公式：`It damages {group's aspect} when {cause}, leading to {consequence}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *It damages **workers' livelihoods** when AI tools replace roles in **journalism, graphic design, and video production**, leading to **structural unemployment** among mid-skilled professionals who lack resources to retrain.*

**Body 2 補充 — [E5「如果不…就會…」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e5如果不就會)**
> 公式：`Without {action}, we face {dire consequence}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *Without **investment in digital retraining programmes**, we face **mass technological unemployment** that disproportionately harms **low-income workers** already vulnerable to economic shocks.*

**Conclusion — [E10「折衷平衡」](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-e10折衷平衡)**
> 公式：`Rather than {extreme}, a balanced approach would be to {middle ground}.`
>
> ✅ 填入示範：
> *Rather than fearing or blindly embracing AI, a balanced approach would be to **regulate its use ethically** while **funding digital literacy programmes** that prepare workers for an automated economy.*
