# 🧱 IELTS Task 2 段落結構公式 — A-R-E + 4C

> 來源：lesson2.md 的 A-R-E 框架 ＋ 4C 微結構整合

---

## 📐 段落結構總覽（6 句 = 1 完整段落）

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  A — Argument（主張）                    ← 1 句     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  R — Reasoning（推理 = 4C 微結構）       ← 4 句     │
│      ├─ C1：Cause（直因）                           │
│      ├─ C2：Consequence（一階後果）                  │
│      ├─ C3：Conflict（虛擬反對 + 反駁）              │
│      └─ C4：Closure（小結 → 鋪橋給 Evidence）        │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  E — Evidence（證據）                    ← 1 句     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
links to [Pseudo_Data_Formula.md](Pseudo_Data_Formula.md)

```

**目標字數：** 80–100 words（一個段落）  
**目標時間：** 約 5–6 分鐘寫完一段

---

## 🔧 逐句公式 + 模板

### 句 1：A — Argument（主張）

> **功能：** 用一句話宣告你的立場 / 這段的核心觀點。  
> **規則：** 必須是「可辯論的主張」，不是背景描述。

**模板：**
```
One significant [advantage / disadvantage / reason] is that [主張].
```

**替換句首：**
- `A key argument in favour of this is that …`
- `The primary reason why … is that …`
- `It is widely acknowledged that …`
- `Perhaps the most compelling argument is that …`

**❌ 錯誤示範：** ~~"Many people think exercise is important."~~（太空泛 = 背景）  
**✅ 正確示範：** "One significant reason why many adults fail to exercise regularly is that modern work culture leaves them physically and mentally drained."

---

### 句 2：C1 — Cause（直因）

> **功能：** 把主張連接到「最靠近的機械原因」。  
> **規則：** 回答「**為什麼**你的主張是對的」— 只推一步，不跳級。

**模板：**
```
This is primarily because [直接原因], which [具體機制].
```

**替換句首：**
- `This is largely due to the fact that …`
- `The root cause lies in …`
- `This stems from …`

**示範：**  
"This is primarily because the standard 9-to-5 schedule, coupled with a lengthy commute, consumes roughly ten hours of their waking day."

---

### 句 3：C2 — Consequence（一階後果）

> **功能：** 說明直因「立即帶來」的客觀結果。  
> **規則：** 用連接詞接住上一句，形成因果鏈的「第二環」。

**模板：**
```
[This / Which] leaves [對象] with [結果], thereby [進一步影響].
```

**替換句構：**
- `As a consequence, [對象] is left with …`
- `This inevitably means that …`
- `, which in turn leads to …`

**示範：**  
"This leaves them with only a narrow evening window for personal obligations, during which domestic responsibilities take priority."

---

### 句 4：C3 — Conflict（虛擬反對 + 即時反駁）

> **功能：** 主動預設反對意見，然後立刻反駁 → 製造第二層因果深度。  
> **規則：** 「讓步 + 反駁」必須在同一句完成，用 `yet / however / nevertheless` 翻轉。

**模板：**
```
Admittedly, [sceptics / critics] may [argue / contend] that [反對理由]; yet [反駁 + 更深層原因].
```

**替換句構：**
- `While some might suggest that …, the reality is that …`
- `It could be argued that …; however, …`
- `Granted, … ; nevertheless, …`

**示範：**  
"Admittedly, sceptics may contend that these adults could simply wake up earlier; yet early-morning workouts demand willpower reserves already depleted by overtime and household duties."

**⚡ 為什麼這句關鍵：**
- 展示 **critical thinking**（考官最看重的）
- 自然增加 **字數和深度**
- 形成 **雙層因果鏈**（Band 7+ 的決定性差距）

---

### 句 5：C4 — Closure（小結 → 鋪橋）

> **功能：** 收攏因果鏈，同時為下一句 Evidence「抹膠水」。  
> **規則：** 結論性語氣 + 讓 Evidence 可以自然接上。

**模板：**
```
As a result, [總結性結論，暗示可被數據驗證].
```

**替換句首：**
- `Consequently, …`
- `It follows, then, that …`
- `This explains why …`
- `For this reason, …`

**示範：**  
"As a result, physical exertion is often the first activity sacrificed when prioritising limited personal time."

---

### 句 6：E — Evidence（證據）

> **功能：** 用「可驗證的數據 / 專名」射擊 Reasoning 的因果細節。  
> **規則：**  
> 1. Evidence 射擊的是 **Reasoning（解釋）**，不是回去重證 Argument（主張）  
> 2. 必須包含 **數字 或 專有名詞**  
> 3. 用「回指詞 + 數據」一句話完成

**模板（回指句構）：**
```
[This + 關鍵詞回指] is evidenced by [數據來源], which found that [具體數字/事實].
```

**替換句構：**
- `For instance, a [年份] [來源] report revealed that [數據].`
- `This pattern is reflected in [專名], where [具體事實].`
- `Indeed, [組織/國家] data shows that [數字].`
- `A striking example of this can be seen in [專名], which [事實].`

**示範：**  
"This pattern is reflected in a 2023 WHO report, which found that 81% of adults in developed nations fail to meet the recommended 150 minutes of weekly exercise."

---

## 📝 完整段落示範

### 題目：Many adults do not do enough physical exercise. What are the reasons and what can be done?

---

#### ✅ 完整段落（6 句 / 98 words）：

> **[A]** One significant reason why many adults fail to exercise regularly is that modern work culture leaves them physically and mentally drained. **[C1]** This is primarily because the standard 9-to-5 schedule, coupled with a lengthy commute, consumes roughly ten hours of their waking day. **[C2]** This leaves them with only a narrow evening window for personal obligations, during which domestic responsibilities take priority. **[C3]** Admittedly, sceptics may contend that these adults could simply wake up earlier; yet early-morning workouts demand willpower reserves already depleted by overtime and household duties. **[C4]** As a result, physical exertion is often the first activity sacrificed when prioritising limited personal time. **[E]** This pattern is reflected in a 2023 WHO report, which found that 81% of adults in developed nations fail to meet the recommended 150 minutes of weekly exercise.

---

### 📊 段落結構拆解：

| # | 角色 | 句子 | 功能 |
|---|------|------|------|
| 1 | **A** — Argument | "One significant reason…drained." | 宣告主張 |
| 2 | **C1** — Cause | "This is primarily because…day." | 直接原因 |
| 3 | **C2** — Consequence | "This leaves them…priority." | 一階後果 |
| 4 | **C3** — Conflict | "Admittedly…duties." | 虛擬反對＋反駁 |
| 5 | **C4** — Closure | "As a result…time." | 收口＋鋪橋 |
| 6 | **E** — Evidence | "This pattern…exercise." | 數據射擊 |

---

## ⏱️ 考場 90 秒寫作流程

```
0-10 秒  → 圈題目關鍵詞 → 腦中閃出 Argument（1 句主張）
10-30 秒 → 問自己「為什麼？」→ 寫 Cause + Consequence（2 句因果鏈）
30-35 秒 → 問自己「反對者會說什麼？」→ 寫 Conflict（讓步＋反駁）
35-50 秒 → 收口寫 Closure → 用 PEST 速搜法找 Evidence
50-90 秒 → 完成 Evidence → 用「雙保險 checklist」快速檢查
```

---

## ✅ 雙保險 Checklist（寫完每段必檢）

| 檢查項目 | ✅ 合格標準 |
|----------|------------|
| **句首回指** | 每句開頭都接住上一句（This / Which / As a result） |
| **因果動詞** | 至少出現 2 個因果動詞（because / leaves / leads to / results in） |
| **不重複主張** | Reasoning ≠ Argument 的 paraphrase |
| **雙層因果** | 至少推進 2 層（cause → consequence 或 conflict → 反駁） |
| **數據存在** | Evidence 含數字 或 專有名詞 |
| **射擊對象正確** | Evidence 射擊 Reasoning 的細節，不是回去證 Argument |

## 🔄 第二個段落示範（完整 essay 會有 2 段 body）

### 題目同上 — 第二段：Solutions

> **[A]** Perhaps the most effective solution would be for employers to integrate physical activity into the working day itself. **[C1]** This is because on-site exercise programmes eliminate the two greatest barriers — time and travel — by bringing fitness directly to the workplace. **[C2]** This means employees can exercise during lunch breaks or between meetings without sacrificing personal evening hours. **[C3]** While critics may argue that such initiatives reduce productivity, research consistently shows that physically active employees take fewer sick days and demonstrate sharper concentration. **[C4]** Consequently, workplace exercise programmes represent an investment that benefits both employers and staff. **[E]** A notable example is the Japanese practice of *rajio taisō* — compulsory workplace stretching — which has been credited with reducing occupational injuries by 25% according to Japan's Ministry of Health.

---

## 🧠 PEST 速搜法（找 Evidence 用）

當腦中一片空白時，用 **PEST** 四個方向快速掃描：

| 字母 | 方向 | 問自己 |
|------|------|--------|
| **P** | Political（政治） | 有哪個政府政策 / 法律 / 國家做過這件事？ |
| **E** | Economic（經濟） | 有什麼花費 / 收入 / GDP 數據？ |
| **S** | Social（社會） | 有什麼社會調查 / 百分比 / 人口數據？ |
| **T** | Technological（科技） | 有什麼 app / 科技工具 / 研究數據？ |

> 💡 **不用真實數據也行！** IELTS 評分標準不要求數據 100% 正確。  
> 只要「合理可信」即可。例如："According to a 2022 national health survey…" 是完全可以接受的。
