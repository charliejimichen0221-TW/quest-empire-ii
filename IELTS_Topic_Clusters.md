# 🎯 IELTS Task 2 — Topic Clusters（論點集群）

**題型縮寫**
| 縮寫 | 題型 |
|---|---|
| `[O]` | Opinion — do you agree / to what extent |
| `[D]` | Discuss — discuss both sides + opinion |
| `[PS]` | Problem-Solution — why + how to solve |
| `[AD]` | Advantage-Disadvantage — pros & cons |
| `[Q]` | Question — open why / what / how |


> **設計理念：** 把 210 道題目歸納為 12 組「論點集群」。
> 只要練熟一個集群的**核心論點（Core Arguments）**，就能應對該集群內所有題目。
> 練一題 ≒ 練多題。

## C1 🏛️ 個人自由 vs 社會責任
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C1](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c1--個人自由-vs-社會責任) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 制度 / 倫理 / 群體](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策)*

> **核心衝突：** 個人有選擇的自由，但這些選擇可能傷害他人或社會。誰來負責？政府管制，還是個人自律？

### 💡 核心論點（練熟這些，以下所有題都能寫）

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **支持管制** | 保護弱勢（兒童、病患、貧困者）比個人自由更重要 |
| **支持管制** | 個人行為有外部成本（healthcare costs, social harm），需要政府介入 |
| **反對管制** | 強制規定剝奪選擇權，會造成反感與反彈 |
| **反對管制** | 個人最清楚自己的需求，教育比管制更有效 |
| **中立折衷** | 提供教育和誘因（tax incentives, awareness campaigns），而非直接禁止 |

### 📋 適用題目（練一組，應付全部）

**〔核心題〕** *從這一題出發，寫完後改寫其他題*
> `[D]` Smoking in public places should be banned. Some people say it protects health; others say it limits freedom. Discuss both views.

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` The government should spend less money on curing illnesses and more on preventing them. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Health care should be free for all people. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Some people think the government should take care of the elderly; others think the family should. Discuss.
- `[O]` Education should be free for everyone. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think the government should not spend on international aid because disadvantaged people exist in their own country. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Some people believe it is the responsibility of individuals to take care of their health and diet. Others believe governments should ensure citizens have a healthy diet. Discuss.
- `[O]` Is personal safety a government responsibility or the responsibility of the individual?
- `[Q]` Safety standards are important when building people's homes. Who should be responsible for enforcing strict building codes?
- `[Q]` Is freedom of speech necessary in a free society?
- `[D]` Nowadays, people believe that governments should invest taxpayers' money in healthcare, while others believe that money should be used in other areas. Discuss.
- `[O]` The government should focus on building new housing for the growing population, not renovating existing old buildings. To what extent do you agree?

## C2 🤖 科技的雙面刃
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C2](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c2--科技的雙面刃) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 科技與數位](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-6-科技與數位)*

> **核心衝突：** 科技帶來效率與便利，但也帶來失業、孤立、安全風險。益大於弊？還是需要管制？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **科技利大** | 提升生產力、醫療水準、教育機會（尤其偏遠地區） |
| **科技利大** | 創造新型工作，雖取代舊工作但淨增就業 |
| **科技害大** | 社交技能下降、人際關係表面化 |
| **科技害大** | 增加網路犯罪、隱私侵害、假訊息散播 |
| **中立折衷** | 科技本身中立，關鍵在於如何監管與教育使用者 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[AD]` Some images and videos online are created by AI. What are the advantages and disadvantages of this?

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` Some people think teachers will one day be replaced in the classroom by technology. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think that the more technology develops, the fewer jobs there will be. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think that AI will one day dominate humanity. To what extent do you agree?
- `[AD]` Most people own a smartphone. What are the benefits? Are there any disadvantages?
- `[AD]` Some countries spend a lot of money on space exploration. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[AD]` We are becoming increasingly dependent on computers. Is this dependence a good thing?
- `[O]` If you want to be successful at work, you should have high-level computer skills. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Modern appliances have become more common in the home. Do you agree that technological advances have improved our lifestyle?
- `[O]` Some people think that modern communication technology is having a negative effect on social relationships. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think that the increasing use of computers and mobile phones hurts young people's reading and writing skills. Do you agree?
- `[Q]` Mobile phones and the internet could have many benefits for older adults. What are the benefits? How can we encourage them to use new technology?
- `[AD]` More and more people are doing business and conducting meetings through video call online. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[D]` Some people think technology development decreases crime; others believe it actually encourages crime. Discuss.
- `[O]` Nowadays, more and more people want to get things done instantly. Why is this? Is it a positive or negative development?

## C3 🏫 教育的目的與方法
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C3](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c3--教育的目的與方法) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 教育與發展](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-7-教育與發展)*

> **核心衝突：** 學術知識 vs 軟實力？學校 vs 家庭？公平教育 vs 品質分化？競爭 vs 合作？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **學術知識派** | 基礎學科（數學、語言、科學）是一切能力的根基 |
| **軟實力派** | 溝通、批判思維、協作才是職場真正需要的 |
| **家庭責任派** | 父母是孩子品格的第一個老師，學校無法取代家庭 |
| **學校責任派** | 學校系統化教育，能給所有兒童平等機會 |
| **競爭派** | 壓力驅動成長，為真實世界做準備 |
| **合作派** | 合作降低焦慮，培養真正適用職場的技能 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[D]` Some people think schools should teach children right from wrong, while other people think this responsibility should fall on the parents. Discuss both sides.

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[D]` Some people believe teachers should be responsible for teaching students to behave ethically. Others argue teachers should focus solely on academic subjects. Discuss.
- `[O]` Some people think that traditional subjects at school are a waste of time because soft skills and experience are more important. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think the best way to get a good job is to have a university education. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Schools should give children more homework to help their academic learning. Do you think this is a good idea?
- `[Q]` As children grow up they spend time both at school and at home. Do you think parents or teachers influence a child most?
- `[O]` Schools should do more to instill discipline in students. Do you think parents should take some responsibility?
- `[D]` Some people think children should be grouped together by ability into classes; others do not agree. Discuss.
- `[O]` Education should be free for everyone. To what extent do you agree?
- `[PS]` Education in rural areas is often not as good as in cities. Why? What can be done?
- `[PS]` Many children think school is boring and this affects their academic results. What solutions can you suggest?
- `[O]` Having a gap year between school and university is a popular choice. Do you think this is a positive trend?
- `[AD]` More and more people are choosing to educate themselves online. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[D]` Competition is better than cooperation in schools. Others believe cooperation leads to better outcomes. Discuss.
- `[O]` Too much emphasis is placed on testing these days. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Some people think that parents should teach children how to be good members of society. Others believe school is the place to learn this. Discuss.
- `[O]` Art classes are as important for children's development as other subjects, so they should be compulsory in high school. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think that Mathematics is the most important subject for school children to study. Discuss both views.
- `[PS]` Many schoolchildren dislike learning maths. What are the reasons? How can we make them enjoy maths?
- `[AD]` Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of learning English at kindergarten.
- `[Q]` Many students choose to attend schools or universities outside their home countries. Why do some students study abroad?
- `[D]` Some students choose to travel before going to university. Others say working experience is more useful. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think that parents should teach children how to be good members of society. Others believe school is the place. Discuss.

## C4 💼 工作、成功與金錢
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C4](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c4--工作成功與金錢) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 經濟與資源](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-3-經濟與資源)*

> **核心衝突：** 熱情 vs 薪資？穩定 vs 彈性？個人努力 vs 系統機會？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **薪資優先** | 金錢提供安全感，讓家庭生活穩定，是基本需求 |
| **熱情優先** | 做有意義的工作讓人更快樂，長期來看反而更成功 |
| **努力決定論** | 努力、自律是成功最可控的因素 |
| **結構決定論** | 出身、人脈、運氣等系統性因素影響成功機率更大 |
| **彈性工作派** | 遠端工作提升效率與生活品質，公司受益 |
| **返回辦公派** | 面對面溝通增強合作、創新、公司文化 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[D]` Some people do work they love; others do work solely for money. Discuss both sides and give your opinion.

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` Working hard is the only way to be successful. Do you agree?
- `[Q]` Why do so many people prefer working remotely from home? Are there any downsides?
- `[Q]` Many people around the world work very long hours. Why? Is this a good thing?
- `[D]` Some companies seek team players; others prefer independent workers. Discuss.
- `[Q]` In many families, both parents work. How does this impact their children? Is this positive or negative?
- `[O]` Large companies should pay CEOs and executives higher salaries than other workers. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Some people argue that it is more important to have an enjoyable job than to earn a lot of money. Discuss.
- `[O]` Successful sports professionals earn much more than nurses, doctors, and teachers. Is this justified?
- `[O]` Many young people's choice of future jobs is based on salary. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?
- `[Q]` An increasing number of people are changing careers during their working life. Why? Is this positive or negative?
- `[O]` Some people are born to be successful. Do you agree?
- `[Q]` Some people work harder than others. Why? Can someone learn how to work hard?
- `[AD]` It is better to work for yourself than for a company. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[PS]` Many young people do not know how to manage their money when graduating. Why? What can be done?
- `[Q]` Many people spend too much money on luxury goods. Why? Is it a positive or negative trend?
- `[PS]` Many people go into personal debt. Why? What can be done?
- `[O]` Many people are spending money rather than saving it. Is this a positive or negative trend?

## C5 🌿 環境、責任與永續
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C5](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c5--環境責任與永續) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 環境與永續](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-5-環境與永續)*

> **核心衝突：** 短期經濟發展 vs 長期環境永續？個人行動 vs 政府/企業責任？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **政府優先** | 個人行動規模太小，需要法規強制企業和國家改變 |
| **個人責任** | 消費者選擇驅動市場，每個人改變生活方式影響深遠 |
| **緊迫性** | 氣候變遷是人類生存威脅，需要立即行動 |
| **經濟折衷** | 環保政策可同時創造綠色就業，不必犧牲經濟 |
| **技術解方** | 再生能源、電動車等技術才是長期解決方案 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[PS]` Climate change is one of the biggest threats facing the world today. What are the causes? What actions should be taken at both the national and individual level?

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[Q]` Do you think it is the government's or the individual's responsibility to protect the environment?
- `[O]` The best way to protect the environment is to use local resources rather than transporting them from other places. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` The international community should ensure all countries reduce the consumption of fossil fuels. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Long-distance flight pollutes the air. We should discourage non-essential flights. To what extent do you agree?
- `[PS]` Problems with environmental pollution have become so severe. Suggest possible solutions.
- `[PS]` More and more cities are struggling with air pollution. Why? What solutions can you suggest?
- `[O]` Electric cars are the way forward to a cleaner world. Do you agree?
- `[PS]` City centres often have traffic congestion. What problems does this cause? How can it be resolved?
- `[D]` Some countries invest more money in public transport; others invest in expanding roads. Discuss.
- `[O]` Budget air travel has become increasingly popular. What problems has this caused?
- `[O]` Increasing the petrol price is the best way to solve the growing traffic and pollution problems. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` It is a natural process for animal species to become extinct. There is no reason why people should try to prevent this. Do you agree?
- `[PS]` Many species are becoming extinct. Do you think this is a problem? What can be done?
- `[O]` Many drug companies test their products on animals. Do you think this is right?
- `[O]` Some people say the fashion industry is bad for the environment. Do you agree?
- `[O]` In the modern world, using animals for food or animal products is no longer necessary. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` A growing number of people feel animals have the same rights as humans. Others argue humans must use animals. Discuss.

## C7 🏥 健康與政府角色
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C7](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c7--健康與政府角色) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 健康與心理](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-4-健康與心理)*

> **核心衝突：** 預防 vs 治療？公費 vs 私費？個人生活方式 vs 政府健康政策？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **預防優先** | 預防疾病成本遠低於治療，且減少個人痛苦 |
| **治療優先** | 疾病發生時，政府必須提供救治，這是基本人權 |
| **公費醫療** | 確保窮人也能獲得醫療，減少不平等 |
| **私費醫療** | 競爭提升品質，公費體制效率低、等待時間長 |
| **生活方式論** | 個人的飲食、運動選擇決定健康，政府只能教育不能強制 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[O]` The government should spend less money on trying to cure illnesses and more money on preventing them. Do you agree?

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` Health care should be free for all people. To what extent do you agree?
- `[AD]` Health services are a basic necessity. Do the advantages of private health care outweigh its disadvantages?
- `[D]` It is the school's responsibility, not the parent's, to teach children what healthy food is. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Schools should increase the number of sports and exercise classes to help children develop into healthy adults. What is your opinion?
- `[O]` Hobbies are healthy activities all adults should have. Do you agree?
- `[AD]` Many people commute to work by car or public transport, but not by cycling or walking. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[Q]` More and more adults are starting to prioritise their mental health. Why? Is this positive or negative?
- `[Q]` Many schools teach team sports. Why?
- `[O]` International sporting events are very popular. Why?
- `[O]` Successful sports professionals earn much more than doctors or nurses. Do you think this is right?
- `[D]` Some people think professional athletes make good role models for young people; others believe they don't. Discuss.
- `[Q]` Some different medical traditions are now widely known. How important is the patient's mental attitude towards treatment?
- `[O]` Some people choose to eat no meat or fish, believing it is better for their health. Discuss this view.

## C8 💰 貧富差距與社會公平
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C8](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c8--貧富差距與社會公平) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 經濟與資源](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-3-經濟與資源)*

> **核心衝突：** 社會公平 vs 市場效率？富國義務 vs 國家自主？再分配 vs 個人責任？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **平等優先** | 貧富差距破壞社會凝聚力，增加犯罪率和不滿情緒 |
| **效率優先** | 貧富分化激勵努力，徵高稅會減少投資和創新 |
| **富國責任** | 發達國家從全球化獲益，有道義幫助貧窮國家 |
| **自主發展** | 外援可能造成依賴，最好的幫助是技術轉移和公平貿易 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[O]` Many people believe that reducing the income gap between the rich and the poor can lead to a happier society. To what extent do you agree?

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` Poor countries need financial aid from richer countries. Do rich countries have an obligation to financially support poorer countries?
- `[O]` Some people think the government should not spend on international aid because disadvantaged people exist in their own country. To what extent do you agree?
- `[PS]` The gap between the rich and the poor is growing. What are the causes of wealth inequality? What measures can reduce it?
- `[O]` Large companies should pay CEOs higher salaries than other workers. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Successful sports professionals can earn much more than nurses, doctors, and teachers. Some think it is fully justified; others believe it is unfair. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Some people think society could benefit more if more people study business than history. To what extent do you agree?
- `[PS]` Many people take on personal debt to fund their lifestyle. Why? What problems does it create?
- `[O]` Nowadays, a person's worth seems to be judged according to social status and material possessions. Old-fashioned values no longer seem important. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` People are living longer than in the past. Why? What problems does this cause?
- `[PS]` Many major cities have a problem with homelessness. How can this be solved?
- `[D]` Migration of workers from rural to urban areas causes both benefits and problems. Discuss.

## C9 🌍 全球化與文化認同
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C9](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c9--全球化與文化認同) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 倫理與自由 / 生活方式](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-8-倫理與自由)*

> **核心衝突：** 世界融合 vs 文化多樣性？觀光帶動經濟 vs 傷害在地文化？單一語言 vs 語言多樣性？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **全球化利** | 交流促進相互理解、科技轉移、貿易繁榮 |
| **全球化弊** | 同質化使文化特色消失，地方語言和傳統消亡 |
| **觀光利** | 帶動就業，提升基礎設施，增加政府收入 |
| **觀光弊** | 破壞自然環境，使在地文化商業化、真實性下降 |
| **語言統一利** | 降低溝通成本，促進全球合作 |
| **語言多樣利** | 每種語言承載獨特文化智慧，不可替代 |

### 📋 適用題目

**〔核心題〕**
> `[O]` As mass communication and transport continue to grow, societies are becoming more and more alike. Some people fear this will lead to a total loss of cultural identity. To what extent do you agree?

**〔同論點延伸題〕**
- `[O]` Globalisation has brought the world closer together. Some people think countries will eventually become very similar. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` The world will one day have only one language. To what extent do you agree?
- `[O]` Children should all learn a foreign language to thrive in today's modern world. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Some people think the world will one day have only one language. Would this be a good thing?
- `[PS]` Traditional lifestyles are slowly being forgotten. Do you think this is a bad thing? What can be done?
- `[Q]` Tourism to remote areas is becoming more popular. Why? What impact does this have on local cultures?
- `[O]` People should be encouraged to take holidays in their own country to boost the economy. To what extent do you agree?
- `[D]` Nearly all cities have museums or art galleries. Some think they are a waste of funds; others disagree. Discuss.
- `[Q]` All countries celebrate national holidays. What role do they play? How important are they?
- `[Q]` Beauty spots are often tourist attractions. What problems does this cause? What can be done?
- `[O]` Social media has brought with it globalisation. Do you think it is a positive or negative trend?
- `[AD]` Traveling in a group with a tour guide is the best way to travel. Do you agree?
- `[D]` People should be encouraged to buy only domestic products; others think we should buy what we prefer. Discuss.

## C10 📱 媒體、網路與資訊
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C10](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c10--媒體網路與資訊) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 科技與數位](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-6-科技與數位)*

> **核心衝突：** 資訊自由 vs 媒體操控？線上連結 vs 人際疏離？網路帶來便利 vs 帶來成癮和危險？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **媒體利** | 即時資訊讓公眾更有知識武裝，更能問責政府 |
| **媒體弊** | 篩選氣泡（filter bubble）使人只聽信自己已有的偏見 |
| **社群媒體利** | 讓偏遠地區、邊緣族群有平台發聲 |
| **社群媒體弊** | 假訊息、網路霸凌、螢幕成癮影響心理健康 |
| **廣告影響** | 廣告製造不必要慾望，促進過度消費 |

- `[AD]` Children enjoy socialising online. Is this a positive or negative trend?
- `[O]` Whoever controls the media also controls the opinions and attitudes of the people; there is little that can be done to rectify this. To what extent do you agree?
- `[Q]` Adverts for products often entice people to buy products they do not need. Why? Is it a problem?
- `[D]` Some people think that advertising may have positive economic effects. Others think it has negative social effects. Discuss.
- `[O]` In some countries, celebrities complain about the way the media publicises their private lives. Some people say they should accept it. Do you agree?
- `[O]` Many celebrities have cosmetic surgery. Is this a positive or negative trend?
- `[O]` More people are becoming famous because of reality shows. Do you think this is a positive or negative trend?
- `[AD]` Many young children look up to actors and actresses as role models. What are the advantages and disadvantages?
- `[O]` Many celebrities complain about their lack of privacy. Do you think celebrities have the right to privacy?

## C11 🎨 藝術、文化與傳統

## C12 🚔 犯罪、法律與道德
*🔗 引擎配方 → [Bridge: C12](Cluster_Hypernym_Bridge.md#c12--犯罪法律與道德) ｜ 🧬 替換詞 → [§1 制度與政策 / 倫理](Hypernym_Cross_Topic_Templates.md#-1-制度與政策)*

> **核心衝突：** 懲罰 vs 改造更生？犯罪是個人選擇還是社會環境造成？嚴格法律 vs 個人自由？

### 💡 核心論點

| 方向 | 核心論點 |
|---|---|
| **懲罰威嚇** | 嚴厲懲罰能有效抑止犯罪，維護社會秩序 |
| **更生改造** | 監獄若只懲罰不改造，出獄後再犯率高 |

- `[O]` Many people believe the arts should be censored. Do you agree?
- `[O]` In some countries, celebrities complain about the way the media publicises their private lives. Should they accept it as part of their fame?

## 📊 集群統計與學習策略

### 每個集群的「最大效益題」

| 集群 | 建議從這一題開始 | 理由 |
|---|---|---|
| C1 | Smoking Ban (Discuss both sides) | 最經典的「管制 vs 自由」辯論框架 |
| C2 | AI-generated content (Adv/Disadv) | 涵蓋所有科技利弊論點 |
| C3 | School vs Parent (moral education) | 觸及教育中最核心的「誰負責」衝突 |
| C4 | Love vs Money (Discuss) | 所有工作題的核心二元對立 |
| C5 | Climate Change (problem-solution) | 環境題的終極母題 |
| C6 | Strict parents vs Freedom (Discuss) | 家庭與兒童議題的根本衝突 |
| C7 | Prevention vs Cure (Opinion) | 最能遷移到其他健康題的論點 |
| C8 | Income gap and happy society | 觸及政策、倫理、經濟三個面向 |
| C9 | Globalisation = loss of culture? | 幾乎所有全球化題的底層論點 |
| C10 | Trust online news? (Discuss) | 媒體題最常見的核心辯論 |
| C11 | Government fund arts? (Discuss) | 觸及「藝術 vs 其他公共支出」優先序 |
| C12 | Reoffending (problem-solution) | 涵蓋懲罰、改造、社會根源三個視角 |

### 學習流程建議

```
Step 1｜選定一個集群
Step 2｜讀懂「核心論點」表格（不用背，理解角度即可）
Step 3｜用「核心題」寫一篇完整 Essay
Step 4｜從延伸題中選 2 題，只改開頭段和結論，其餘論點微調
Step 5｜比較三篇文章的論點異同，找出「可移植的句子」
→ 重複以上流程，12 個集群 = 36 篇 Essay 训練量，覆蓋 200+ 題！
```
