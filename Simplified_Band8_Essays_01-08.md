## ✏️ Essay 1 — Smoking Ban in Public Places

**題目：** In many countries, smoking is now illegal in public places. Many people believe that such a ban is justified. Do you agree or disagree?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Safety/Body):** Smoking hurts non-smokers (passive smoking).
2. **Money/Economy (Government Spending):** Treating sick smokers costs the government money.

**Essay:**

Smoking in public places is a big problem in many cities. Today, many governments have decided to ban smoking in areas like parks and restaurants. I completely agree with this rule because it protects the safety of the public and saves money for the government.

First, banning smoking is good for public health. When a person smokes, the smoke spreads to other people nearby. This is called passive smoking. It is very harmful to the body and can cause serious illnesses. **For example, breathing in other people's smoke can damage the lungs and even cause cancer in children who are playing nearby.** If we stop people from smoking in public, we protect these innocent people.

Second, this law helps the economy by saving tax money. Treating diseases caused by smoking causes a lot of cost for the country. The government spends millions of dollars on hospitals and doctors every year to treat smokers. **For instance, treating a patient with heart disease or lung problems in a public hospital is very expensive for the state.** If fewer people get sick from smoke, the government can save this money and use it for schools or roads.

In conclusion, I support the ban on smoking in public places. It is good for our health because it keeps the air clean, and it is good for the economy because it reduces healthcare costs.

## ✏️ Essay 2 — Competition vs Cooperation in Children

**題目：** Some people think that a sense of competition in children should be encouraged. Others believe that children who are taught to cooperate rather than compete become more useful adults. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Mind/Stress):** Competition causes stress and unhappiness.
2. **Money/Economy (Jobs/Income):** Cooperation is needed for jobs and business success.

**Essay:**

Some people think children should learn to compete to be successful. However, others believe that cooperation is more important. In my opinion, teaching children to work together is better for their future adult lives.

On the one hand, competition can be stressful for children. If they always feel they must win, they might feel unhappy when they lose. This is bad for their minds. **For example, a student who gets a low score in a test might lose confidence and feel sad compared to the top student.** This pressure can hurt their mental health and make them dislike school.

On the other hand, learning to cooperate is very useful for future jobs. In most businesses today, employees need to work in a team to solve problems. **For instance, in a software company, engineers and designers must work together to build a new app.** If a person knows how to cooperate, they will be a better worker and help the company make money. This skill leads to a better career and income.

In conclusion, while competition is common, I believe teaching cooperation is better. It protects children's mental well-being and prepares them for good jobs in the future.

## ✏️ Essay 3 — Childcare: Family vs Daycare Centres

**題目：** Some parents think that childcare centres provide the best services for children of preschool age. Other working parents think that family members such as grandparents are better caregivers. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Society/Relationship (Family time/Culture):** Grandparents provide love and culture.
2. **Society/Relationship (Education/Friends):** Centres provide education and friends.

**Essay:**

Parents have different choices for looking after their young children. Some prefer asking family members like grandparents, while others choose childcare centres. I think childcare centres are better because they offer good education.

Family members can give a lot of love to the child. When grandparents look after the children, it increases family time. **For example, a grandmother can tell traditional stories to the child, which helps them learn about their family culture.** The children can feel safe and loved in this environment. Also, for many families, this is a happy way to raise kids without spending money.

However, childcare centres are better for learning. Teachers in these centres are trained to educate young children. They teach them how to read, draw, and play safely. **For instance, children in a centre learn how to share toys and play games with many other children, which is not possible at home.** This helps them learn social skills, which are important for their future life in the community.

In conclusion, although family care is full of love, I think childcare centres are better. They provide structured education and help children make friends.

## ✏️ Essay 4 — Life Was Better with Simpler Technology

**題目：** Life was better when technology was simpler. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Safety/Life Expectancy):** Medical tech saves lives.
2. **Money/Economy (Business/Income):** Tech makes work faster and creates income.

**Essay:**

Some people think life was better in the past when technology was simple. I strongly disagree with this idea. I believe modern technology has improved our health and our economy.

First, technology makes our lives safer and longer. In the past, many simple diseases could kill people. Now, we have modern hospitals and machines that can cure sickness. **For example, doctors can now use advanced machines to find and cure cancer early, saving many lives.** This increases life expectancy. People live longer and healthier lives today because of this progress.

Second, technology helps businesses. Computers and the internet make work faster and easier. Companies can sell products to the whole world, not just their local town. **For instance, a small shop owner can use a website to sell clothes to customers in other countries.** This creates more jobs and increases income for workers. Without technology, business would be slow and limited.

In conclusion, life is better now because of technology. It improves our medical safety and helps businesses make more money.

## ✏️ Essay 5 — Media's Impact on Society

**題目：** At present, the media affects people's lives significantly. What impact does this have on society? Is it a negative or positive development?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Mind/Stress):** Bad news causes fear and stress.
2. **Society/Relationship (Crime/Safety):** Violence in media leads to crime.

**Essay:**

The media, such as TV and the internet, is everywhere in our daily lives. In my opinion, it has a negative effect on society because it causes mental stress and can increase crime.

One big problem is that the news often focuses on bad events. When we watch the news, we often see stories about wars, accidents, and disasters. **For example, watching reports about a dangerous virus or a terrible war every night can make people feel very anxious.** This fear is bad for our minds. People might worry too much about their safety and feel unhappy.

Another problem is violence in movies and video games. Many action movies show people fighting and killing. **For instance, young children who play violent video games might think that hitting others is a normal way to solve problems.** This can lead to more crime in the community. It makes our schools and neighborhoods less safe for everyone.

In conclusion, the media often has a negative impact. It hurts our mental health by showing scary news, and it can encourage violence in our society.

## ✏️ Essay 6 — Money as Main Reason to Stay in a Job

**題目：** Some believe that money is the main reason people stay working for the same company. Do you agree or disagree?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Happiness/Mind):** Happiness is more important than money.
2. **Society/Relationship (Family time):** Time with family is essential.

**Essay:**

Some people say that money is the main reason why employees stay with the same company. I disagree. I think happiness and family time are more important reasons.

First, money cannot buy happiness. If a person hates their job or their boss, a high salary will not make them happy. Stress at work is very bad for the mind. **For example, a worker who is treated badly by their manager will likely leave the company, even if the pay is good.** Feeling happy and relaxed at work is often more important for an employee than the monthly paycheck.

Second, family time is very important. Some jobs pay a lot of money but require working late every night. This takes time away from family and children. **For instance, a father might choose to stay in a lower-paying job simply because it allows him to have dinner with his children every evening.** A good balance between work and family life is a strong reason to stay in a job.

In conclusion, money is important, but it is not the only reason. Happiness and time for family are often the main reasons people stay in their jobs.

## ✏️ Essay 7 — Who Should Pay for Elderly Care

**題目：** Who do you think should pay for elderly care, the government or the family?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Money/Economy (Government Spending/Tax):** People pay taxes, so govt should pay back.
2. **Money/Economy (Cost/Poverty):** Families are too poor to pay.

**Essay:**

Who should pay for the care of old people? I believe using government spending is better than asking the family to pay.

The main reason is that people pay taxes all their lives. Every worker gives part of their income to the government. This money should be used to help citizens when they are old. **For example, in many European countries, the tax people pay during their working life is saved to pay for their hospital care when they retire.** It is fair that the government gives this help back.

Another reason is that care is very expensive. Most families do not have enough savings to pay for a nursing home or a private nurse. **For instance, a room in a good nursing home can cost thousands of dollars a month, which is much more than a normal family earns.** If families have to pay, they might become poor. They might not have enough money for food or education.

In conclusion, the government should pay for elderly care. Tax money should be used for this purpose so that families do not suffer from high costs.

## ✏️ Essay 8 — Childhood Obesity

**題目：** Obesity is a major disease prevalent among children. What are its causes, and what solutions can be offered?

**選用 Ideas：**
1. **Health/Well-being (Body/Food):** Bad food (sugar/fat).
2. **Society/Relationship (Environment/Sedentary):** No exercise only games.
3. **Society/Relationship (Education):** Treat with knowledge.
4. **Society/Relationship (Family time):** Exercise with family.

**Essay:**

Many children today are becoming overweight. This essay will look at the causes of this problem and suggest some solutions.

The main cause is the modern lifestyle and food. Many children eat fast food because it is cheap and tasty. However, this food has too much sugar and fat. **For example, drinking sugary soda and eating hamburgers every day will make a child gain weight very quickly.** Also, children do not move enough. **For instance, instead of playing football outside, many kids sit in their rooms playing video games for hours.** This makes them lazy and unhealthy.

To solve this, education and family activity are important. Schools should teach students about healthy food. **For example, teachers can show students how much sugar is in a can of cola.** Also, families should spend more time doing physical activities together. **For instance, parents can take their children to the park on weekends to run or cycle.** This helps children move their bodies and stay fit.

In conclusion, bad food and a lazy lifestyle cause obesity. However, better education and family activities can help solve this problem.
