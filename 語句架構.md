# 🏗️ High Coherence & Cohesion: The "Blank-Filling" Sentence Architecture
> **概念：** 專為 [The_Thinking_Formula.md] 設計的「萬能填充句型」。
> **核心：** 使用 **Hypernyms (上義詞)**（如 "This factor", "Such a trend"）作為連接詞，確保即使當下想不到具體單字，也能寫出流暢、邏輯嚴密的 Band 7+ 句子。

---

## 🎯 1. Opinion / Agree or Disagree (觀點題)
> **Goal:** Clearly state your side and support it with H-M-S reasons.

### Body Paragraph (Supporting Your View)
*   **Topic Sentence:** The primary reason for supporting this view is related to **[Category: HEALTH / MONEY / SOCIETY]**.
*   **Reason (Hypernym):** Specifically, **this [trend / policy / phenomenon]** stems from human nature, as people are naturally **[lazy / greedy / creative / curious]**.
*   **Pros/Effect:** Consequently, **such a development** leads to beneficial outcomes, such as making life **[easier / richer / safer]** for the general public.
*   **Example:** A clear instance of this can be seen in **[Example: Apple / Tesla / Japan]**, where **[users / citizens]** have greatly benefited.

### Body Paragraph (Concession/Opposing View)
*   **Topic Sentence:** On the other hand, critics argue that there are significant drawbacks concerning **[Category: COST / SAFETY / TRADITION]**.
*   **Cons (Hypernym):** The most concerning aspect is that **this approach** effectively destroys **[Target: health / traditions / the environment]**, which is a high price to pay.
*   **Solution:** Therefore, the government must intervene to **[tax / ban / regulate]** **these harmful activities** to mitigate the damage.

---

## ⚖️ 2. Discussion (Both Views) (討論題)
> **Goal:** Objectively analyze two sides (Site A vs Side B).

### Body A (View 1 - Positive)
*   **Topic Sentence:** Proponents of this idea often emphasize the advantages regarding **[Category: ECONOMY / INNOVATION / CONVENIENCE]**.
*   **Reason:** This is largely because **modern technology** allows **[entities: companies / workers]** to operate more efficiently.
*   **Pros (Hypernym):** As a result, **these advancements** create substantial **[profit / jobs]**, boosting the overall **[economy / standard of living]**.

### Body B (View 2 - Negative)
*   **Topic Sentence:** Conversely, opponents raise valid concerns about the impact on **[Category: WELL-BEING / CULTURE / EQUALITY]**.
*   **Phenomena:** In many cases, we observe **[disturbing scenes]** such as **[Phenomena: dirty smoke / phone addiction / long queues]**.
*   **Cons (Hypernym):** **Such negative consequences** suggest that **this trend** causes unnecessary **[stress / pollution / debt]** and should be reconsidered.

---

## ❓ 3. Problem & Solution (問題解決題)
> **Goal:** Identify the cause (Reason) and propose a fix (Solution).

### Body A (The Problem)
*   **Topic Sentence:** One of the most pressing issues today is the decline in **[Category: PUBLIC HEALTH / SOCIAL VALUES]**.
*   **Reason (Hypernym):** The root cause of **this dilemma** is that modern society has become too **[fast / materialistic / competitive]**.
*   **Effect:** If left unchecked, **this negligence** will inevitably lead to **[severe consequences]** such as **[disease / crime / climate disaster]**.

### Body B (The Solution)
*   **Topic Sentence:** To address **this challenge**, immediate action is required from both the government and individuals.
*   **Solution 1 (Force):** primarily, the authorities should impose strict rules to **[tax / ban / fund]** **the underlying causes**.
*   **Solution 2 (Education):** Furthermore, schools must take responsibility to **[teach / educate]** the younger generation about **[proper values / healthy habits]**.

## 📈 4. Advantages & Disadvantages (優缺點題)
> **Goal:** Weigh the benefits against the drawbacks.

### Body A (The Benefits)
*   **Topic Sentence:** There are undeniable benefits to **[Topic]**, primarily in terms of **[Category: CONVENIENCE / FINANCE]**.
*   **Pros (Hypernym):** The most obvious advantage is that **this facility** makes daily tasks much **[easier / faster]** for **[users / consumers]**.
*   **Example:** Take **[Example: Smartphones / Cars]** as an example; they have revolutionized how we live.

### Body B (The Drawbacks)
*   **Topic Sentence:** However, the negative implications for **[Category: MENTAL HEALTH / ENVIRONMENT]** cannot be ignored.
*   **Cons (Hypernym):** A major downside is that **excessive use** of **these tools** often leads to **[isolation / pollution / illness]**.
*   **Effect:** Over time, **such a detrimental effect** could outweigh the initial benefits.

---

## 🔄 5. Two-Part Question (混合題)
> **Goal:** Directly answer two specific questions (e.g., "Why?" and "Is it good?").

### Body A (Answering "Why?")
*   **Topic Sentence:** The primary reason for **this emerging trend** is rooted in **[human nature / technological progress]**.
*   **Reason:** Simply put, people are naturally **[lazy / curious / greedy]** and constantly seek ways to improve their comfort.
*   **Phenomena:** This is evident when we see **[Phenomena: rising prices / crowded cities]** becoming the new normal.

### Body B (Answering "Is it positive?")
*   **Topic Sentence:** In my opinion, this is a largely **[positive / negative]** development for society.
*   **Pros/Cons:** While it may bring **[short-term convenience]**, the long-term cost to **[Category: HEALTH / CULTURE]** is too high.
*   **Conclusion Sentence:** Therefore, unless we **[Solution: regulate / educate]**, the situation may worsen.

## 🗝️ Key Hypernyms for "Blank Moments" (萬能代名詞)
> 當你想不到 "Smartphone", "Pollution", "Tax" 這些具體詞時，請用以下單字代替：

*   **Positive Things:** This development / This innovation / This facility / These advancements.
*   **Negative Things:** This dilemma / This negligence / Such a detrimental effect / These harmful activities.
*   **People:** Users / Consumers / Citizens / The younger generation / The general public.
*   **Actions:** This approach / This trend / Such a practice / This policy.
