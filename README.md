# ⚔️ Quest Empire II: The Forge 
*(IELTS Essay Planner / 雅思寫作鍛造爐)*

[English](#english) | [中文](#chinese)

---

<h2 id="english">🇬🇧 English</h2>

**Quest Empire II: The Forge** is an interactive, step-by-step IELTS Task 2 essay writing application designed to help learners systematically build high-scoring paragraphs. By treating essay components as "engines" and "weapons," it transforms IELTS preparation into an engaging crafting process.

### ✨ Key Features
- **Intelligent Topic Analysis**: Paste any IELTS Task 2 question, and the app connects it to one of the 12 core clusters.
- **Argument Selection**: Choose from predefined, high-quality arguments tailored to the specific topic cluster.
- **Vocabulary Extraction**: Access topic-specific vocabulary instantly.
- **Paragraph Engines (Formulas)**: Assemble your essay structure using pre-built "engines" (e.g., Cause & Effect, Contrast, Example) ensuring logical flow.
- **AI-Powered Draft Generation**: Powered by Kimi AI, it generates a complete bilingual draft using the selected engines and vocabulary. The English output is specifically tuned to CEFR A2-B2 vocabulary to ensure comprehensibility for intermediate learners.
- **Instant Bilingual Switching**: The entire UI and AI-generated content can toggle instantly between English and Traditional Chinese.

### 🚀 How to Use
1. **① Topic**: Paste your essay question.
2. **② Cluster**: Confirm or select the closest topic cluster.
3. **③ Arguments**: Handpick 2-3 core arguments for your essay.
4. **④ Vocab**: Review the recommended vocabulary for those arguments.
5. **⑤ Engine**: Select the paragraph structures (engines) you want to use.
6. **⑥ Assemble & Write**: Review your essay skeleton and click "AI Write" to let the Kimi AI forge your final bilingual paragraphs!

<h2 id="chinese">🇹🇼 中文</h2>

**Quest Empire II: The Forge**（雅思寫作鍛造爐）是一款互動式、逐步引導的 IELTS Task 2 寫作工具。它將抽象的文章段落轉化為「引擎」與「武器」，幫助學習者系統化地組裝出高分文章，讓雅思備考過程變得像打造專屬裝備一樣有趣。

### ✨ 核心功能
- **智能題型分析**：貼上任何雅思 Task 2 題目，系統會自動歸類至 12 大核心學科集群。
- **論點挑選**：快速從各集群中挑選預先準備好的高質量核心論點。
- **主題詞彙速查**：提供對應特定論點的關鍵詞彙。
- **段落引擎（句型公式）**：利用預設的「引擎」（如：因果推導、正反對比、實例佐證）來架構你的段落，確保寫作邏輯嚴密。
- **AI 智能生成初稿**：串接 Kimi AI，根據你挑選的論點、詞彙及引擎，自動生成中英雙語的完整段落。英文版本特別設定使用 CEFR A2-B2 等級的單字，讓中階學習者更容易理解與吸收。
- **中英雙語即時切換**：整個操作介面與 AI 生成的內容皆支援中英文即時切換。

### 🚀 使用流程
1. **① 貼題目**：貼上你遇到的雅思 Task 2 寫作題目。
2. **② 選集群**：確認或手動選擇最接近的題型集群。
3. **③ 選論點**：挑選 2-3 個你想要在段落中使用的核心論點方向。
4. **④ 查詞彙**：瀏覽系統推薦的主題專屬詞彙。
5. **⑤ 選引擎**：選擇你要用來組裝段落的幾何/句型公式。
6. **⑥ 組裝與生成**：檢視組合好的文章骨架，點擊「AI 寫段落」讓 Kimi AI 為你鍛造出完整的雙語初稿！
