# 🧠 終極思考公式：考場腦袋空白時的「自動生成引擎」

> **目標：** 從 22 篇 Band 8+ 範文中歸納蒸餾出一套完整的「填空題式」思考公式。
> 即使你在考場腦袋完全空白，只要按下面的公式填空，就能瞬間生成：
> **(0) 2 Reasons｜(1) 2 Pros + 2 Examples｜(2) 2 Cons + 2 Examples｜(3) 2 Phenomena｜(4) 2 Effects｜(5) 2 Solutions**

---

## 📐 核心框架：H-M-S 三角定位法

> 拿到任何題目，**第一步永遠是**：這個題目跟 **Health / Money / Society** 哪兩個有關？

```
        ┌─────────────┐
        │  🏥 HEALTH  │  身體 (Cancer/Pain) ＋ 心理 (Stress/Happy)
        └──────┬──────┘
               │
    ┌──────────┼──────────┐
    │                     │
┌───┴───┐           ┌────┴────┐
│💰MONEY│           │🤝SOCIETY│
└───────┘           └─────────┘
 家庭(Bill/Rent)     犯罪(Safety)
 企業(Profit)        環境(Pollution)
 政府(Tax/Budget)    教育(Skill/Value)
                     文化(Heritage/Art)
                     自由(Freedom/Rights)
                     品格(Character/Values)
```

**操作方式：** 選定兩個領域 → 分別套用下面的 6 組公式引擎 → 完成！

## 引擎零：REASONS（原因解釋生成器）❓

> **核心提問：** *「為什麼會這樣？人性的哪個弱點/優點導致了這件事？」（Why do people do it?）*
> **適用題型：** "Why is this?" / "Why is that?" / "What are the reasons?"

### 📌 公式 A：「人性本能」路線

**模板：**
```
Because humans are naturally [lazy / greedy / creative / curious / social / emotional],
they tend to [具體行為].
```

**觸發思路：** 人為什麼做這件事？→ 因為人天生就是 **懶的 / 愛美的 / 怕無聊的 / 需要歸屬感的**

| 情境 | 填空示範 |
|:---|:---|
| 為什麼支持藝術 | Because humans are naturally **creative and emotional**, they tend to **seek beauty and self-expression through art**. |
| 為什麼買名牌 | Because humans are naturally **social and status-driven**, they tend to **buy luxury goods to impress others**. |
| 為什麼抗拒改變 | Because humans are naturally **afraid of the unknown**, they tend to **stay in their comfort zone**. |
| 為什麼沉迷手機 | Because humans are naturally **curious and easily distracted**, they tend to **check notifications constantly**. |
| 為什麼喜歡藝術嗜好 | Because humans are naturally **emotional beings who need relaxation**, they tend to **use painting or music to relieve stress**. |
| 為什麼孩子不愛閱讀 | Because humans are naturally **drawn to visual stimulation and instant reward**, children tend to **prefer videos and games over books**. |
| 為什麼人們亂花錢 | Because humans are naturally **impulsive and seek instant gratification**, they tend to **spend money now rather than save for the future**. |
| 為什麼買奢侈品 | Because humans are naturally **competitive and crave social approval**, they tend to **buy luxury goods to show off their status**. |
| 為什麼想當老闆 | Because humans are naturally **independence-seeking and ambitious**, they tend to **dream of being their own boss**. |
| 為什麼有人更努力 | Because humans are naturally **wired differently in discipline and motivation** — some inherit strong willpower, while others need **external pressure like deadlines or competition** to push them. |
| 為什麼跟隨趨勢 | Because humans are naturally **social creatures who fear being excluded**, they tend to **copy what the majority does to feel accepted**. |
| 為什麼不尊重老人 | Because humans are naturally **attracted to youth and novelty**, they tend to **value speed and technology over patience and experience**. |
| 為什麼犯罪 | Because humans are naturally **driven by survival instincts and short-term desires**, some tend to **steal or cheat when they feel desperate or see an easy opportunity**. |
| 為什麼愛看犯罪節目 | Because humans are naturally **fascinated by danger and mystery from a safe distance**, they tend to **enjoy crime shows that provide thrills without real risk**. |
| 為什麼想去偏遠旅遊 | Because humans are naturally **curious about the unknown and crave new experiences**, they tend to **seek remote, untouched destinations to escape routine life**. |
| 為什麼孩子覺得學校無聊 | Because humans are naturally **wired for play and exploration**, children tend to **find sitting in a classroom listening to lectures extremely boring**. |
| 為什麼想要 Gap Year | Because humans are naturally **restless and need real-world experiences to grow**, young people tend to **want a break between school and university to discover themselves**. |
| 為什麼亂丟垃圾 | Because humans are naturally **lazy and short-sighted**, they tend to **drop litter when no one is watching rather than walk to a bin**. |
| 為什麼不想生孩子 | Because humans are naturally **driven by self-interest and personal fulfilment**, some women tend to **prioritise career, travel, and freedom over raising children**. |
| 為什麼孩子愛吃垃圾食物 | Because humans are naturally **drawn to sugar, salt, and fat**, children tend to **choose burgers, chips, and sweets over vegetables and home-cooked meals**. |
| 為什麼孩子愛玩螢幕 | Because humans are naturally **wired for instant stimulation and dopamine rewards**, children tend to **spend hours on games and videos rather than going outside to play**. |
| 為什麼運動員賺很多 | Because humans are naturally **drawn to excitement and hero worship**, fans tend to **pay huge amounts for tickets and merchandise**, making top athletes worth billions to sponsors. |
| 為什麼愛網購 | Because humans are naturally **impatient and love convenience**, they tend to **choose one-click shopping from their sofa over queuing in crowded stores**. |
| 為什麼愛看真人秀 | Because humans are naturally **fascinated by drama and other people's lives**, they tend to **binge-watch reality shows that offer scandal, conflict, and emotional outbursts**. |
| 為什麼人們怕AI | Because humans are naturally **afraid of losing control**, they tend to **fear machines that can think faster, learn independently, and eventually make decisions without human input**. |
| 為什麼過度加班 | Because humans are naturally **driven by fear of failure and desire for recognition**, they tend to **work longer hours than necessary to prove their worth and outperform colleagues**. |
| 為什麼想搬到鄉下 | Because humans are naturally **drawn to peace, open space, and clean air**, many city dwellers tend to **dream of escaping the noise, pollution, and overcrowding of urban life**. |

### 📌 公式 B：「系統/環境推動」路線

**模板：**
```
Because [the government / schools / technology / media / the economy] [promotes / enables / forces / fails to address] [某事],
people have [no choice but to / every reason to] [行為].
```

**觸發思路：** 是什麼外在力量造成的？→ **政府推動？科技改變？媒體洗腦？經濟壓力？教育缺失？**

| 為什麼老人不被尊重 | Because **technology changes so fast that older people struggle to keep up**, society tends to **see them as outdated and irrelevant**. |
| 為什麼有人成功 | Because **wealthy families provide better education, networks, and financial safety nets**, children from rich backgrounds have **every advantage to succeed**. |
| 為什麼犯罪率上升 | Because **poverty, unemployment, and broken families leave people without hope or support**, some have **no choice but to turn to crime to survive**. |
| 為什麼再犯率高 | Because **prisons focus on punishment rather than education or job training**, released criminals have **no skills and no job prospects**, so they reoffend. |

| 為什麼偏遠旅遊熱門 | Because **cheap flights and travel influencers on Instagram promote hidden destinations**, tourists have **every reason to explore remote areas that were once inaccessible**. |
| 為什麼鄉村教育較差 | Because **governments invest most education budgets in cities**, rural schools have **fewer qualified teachers, outdated textbooks, and no internet access**. |

| 為什麼物種滅絕 | Because **deforestation, pollution, and urban expansion destroy natural habitats**, animals have **nowhere to live, breed, or find food**. |

| 為什麼家人不再一起吃飯 | Because **both parents work long hours and children have after-school activities**, families have **no overlapping free time to sit down for a meal together**. |
| 為什麼家人缺乏溝通 | Because **smartphones and social media absorb everyone's attention**, family members have **every reason to scroll their screens instead of talking to each other**. |
| 為什麼傳統食物消失 | Because **fast food chains offer cheap, quick meals with massive advertising budgets**, traditional restaurants have **no way to compete on speed or price**. |
| 為什麼垃圾食物流行 | Because **junk food companies spend billions on ads with cartoon characters and toy giveaways**, children have **every reason to demand fast food over home-cooked vegetables**. |
| 為什麼人們開車上班 | Because **cities are designed for cars with wide highways and free parking**, people have **no incentive to cycle or walk** when driving is **faster, easier, and more comfortable**. |
| 為什麼重視心理健康 | Because **social media, job insecurity, and the pandemic have increased anxiety and depression**, people have **finally recognised that mental health is just as important as physical health**. |
| 為什麼線上會議流行 | Because **video conferencing tools like Zoom and Teams are free and easy to use**, companies have **every reason to replace expensive business trips with a 30-minute online call**. |
| 為什麼報紙消失 | Because **free online news is available 24/7 and updated every minute**, young people have **no reason to pay for a newspaper that is already outdated by the time it's printed**. |
| 為什麼廣告有效 | Because **companies use psychology, algorithms, and influencers to target vulnerable consumers**, people have **every reason to buy things they don't need** when ads make them feel **incomplete without the product**. |
| 為什麼科技取代工作 | Because **robots and AI can work 24/7 without salaries, sick days, or mistakes**, companies have **every financial incentive to automate factories, warehouses, and even customer service**. |
| 為什麼花錢探索太空 | Because **governments compete for technological prestige and scientific breakthroughs**, space agencies receive **billions in funding** even when **citizens struggle with poverty and healthcare**. |

| 為什麼工人進城 | Because **cities offer higher wages, better hospitals, and more job opportunities**, rural workers have **every incentive to migrate** even if it means **living in cramped, expensive conditions**. |
| 為什麼無家可歸 | Because **housing prices have skyrocketed while wages stagnate, and mental illness and addiction remain undertreated**, people have **no safety net when they lose a job or a family**, ending up **on the streets**. |

## 一、60 秒 SOP（標準作業程序）

```
⏱️ 0-5 秒     → 讀題，鎖定 H-M-S 中的兩個領域
⏱️ 5-15 秒    → 引擎一：用 Pro A + Pro B 想兩個好處
⏱️ 15-25 秒   → 引擎二：用 Con A + Con B 想兩個壞處
⏱️ 25-35 秒   → 引擎三：用 Phenomena A + B 想兩個畫面
⏱️ 35-45 秒   → 引擎四：用 Effect A + B 想兩個未來
⏱️ 45-55 秒   → 引擎五：用 Solution A + B 想兩個解法
⏱️ 55-60 秒   → 為 Pro 和 Con 各配一對 Example（品牌+個人）
```

## 五、公式記憶口訣（考前 30 秒默念）

```
🧠 R-S-M-I-L-E-S 口訣

R = Reasons（Why: 為什麼？→ 人性本能 + 系統推動）
S = Smiles（Pro: 誰笑了？→ 個人受益 + 經濟受益 + 精神/文化價值）
M = Moans（Con: 誰哭了？→ 身心傷害 + 經濟損失 + 自由受限）
I = Images（Phenomena: 拍照看到什麼？→ 街頭畫面 + 新聞數據）
L = Later（Effect: 10年後怎樣？→ 美好未來 + 恐怖未來）
E = Emergency（Solution: 打電話叫誰救？→ 政府立法 + 學校教育 + 折衷平衡）
S = Specific（Example: 說個名字！→ 品牌/國家 + 個人故事）
```

> **⚠️ 最後提醒：** 每個引擎都有 **A（個人/身心/場景）**、**B（經濟/系統/數據）**、**C（文化/自由/折衷）** 三條路線。
> 考場上只要記住：**一條走「人」，一條走「錢」，一條走「價值/自由」**，就永遠能覆蓋任何題目！
