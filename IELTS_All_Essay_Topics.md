## 🧠 Idea Expansion Band 8 — Essays 1–22

> 每道題目對應一份完整的 Idea Expansion，內含 7 維度論點 (Pros/Cons/Reasons/Phenomena/Effects/Solutions/Examples)

### 📘 Essays 1–8

1. `[D]` Smoking in public places should be banned. Some people agree it protects health, while others argue it limits personal freedom. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
2. `[D]` Some people think competition is better than cooperation in schools. Others believe cooperation leads to better outcomes. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
3. `[D]` Some people think children should be raised by family members at home; others think sending them to childcare centres is better. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
4. `[D]` Some people believe that simpler technology is better than modern, complex technology. Others argue the opposite. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
5. `[PS]` The media — including news and social platforms — has a large impact on people's lives. What problems does this cause? What solutions can you suggest?
6. `[D]` Some people think money is the most important factor in choosing a job. Others believe job satisfaction is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
7. `[D]` Some people think the government should be responsible for caring for the elderly; others believe it is a family responsibility. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
8. `[PS]` Childhood obesity is becoming a serious problem in many countries. What are the causes? What measures can be taken to address this issue?

### 📘 Essays 9–16

9. `[D]` Some people think that children should be allowed to do paid work from an early age; others believe it harms their education and development. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
10. `[PS]` Air pollution is a serious problem in many cities. What are the main causes? What measures can governments and individuals take to reduce it?
11. `[PS]` Many people take on personal debt to fund their lifestyle. Why is this happening? What problems does it create, and how can it be addressed?
12. `[D]` Some people think that children should be taught to be resilient so they can face difficulties. Others think protecting children from hardship is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
13. `[D]` Some people think museums serve an important role in society and should receive government funding. Others believe the money should be spent elsewhere. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
14. `[D]` Some people believe that job satisfaction is more important than a high salary. Others disagree. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
15. `[AD]` Social media allows people to connect and share information freely. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using social media?
16. `[PS]` The internet has created new risks, including cybercrime and identity theft. Why is this happening? What can governments and individuals do to stay safe?

### 📘 Essays 17–22

17. `[Q]` In many countries, more and more people are choosing to live alone. Why is this? Is it a positive or negative development for society?
18. `[PS]` The gap between the rich and the poor is growing in many countries. What are the causes of wealth inequality? What measures can reduce it?
19. `[D]` Some people think that the government, not the individual, should be responsible for protecting people's well-being. Others disagree. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

### 🎓 Education & Learning (教育)
